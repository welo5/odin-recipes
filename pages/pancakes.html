Banana Pancakes

These are sweet, fluffy pancakes that are made with ripe bananas. Theyâ€™re a great way to use up overripe bananas and make a delicious breakfast or brunch dish. The bananas not only add flavor but also make the pancakes naturally sweet. They can be topped with syrup, fresh fruit, or anything else you like.

Ingredients:

  • Bananas
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Mash the bananas and mix with eggs, baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan and pour in some batter.
  3. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook the other side.
  4. Serve with your favorite toppings.
