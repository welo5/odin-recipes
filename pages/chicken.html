Chicken Stir Fry

This is a versatile and colorful dish that originates from China. It typically includes bite-sized pieces of chicken and a variety of vegetables, all cooked quickly in a hot pan or wok with a savory sauce. Itâ€™s a great way to use up leftover vegetables and can be served with rice for a complete meal.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Bell peppers
  • Onion
  • Soy sauce
  • Cornstarch
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Cut the chicken into thin slices and marinate with soy sauce and cornstarch.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and stir-fry the chicken until cooked through.
  3. Remove the chicken and stir-fry sliced bell peppers and onion until tender.
  4. Add the chicken back to the pan, stir well, and serve.
