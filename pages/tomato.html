Tomato and Basil Bruschetta

This is a classic Italian appetizer that features fresh tomatoes, basil, and garlic on top of toasted bread slices. The tomatoes are usually diced and mixed with the other ingredients, then spooned onto the bread. It’s a refreshing and easy-to-make starter that’s perfect for summer gatherings.

Ingredients:

  • Baguette
  • Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Dice the tomatoes and chop the basil.
  2. Mix tomatoes, basil, minced garlic, olive oil, and salt.
  3. Slice the baguette and toast until golden.
  4. Top each slice with the tomato mixture and serve.
